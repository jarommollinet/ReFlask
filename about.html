How it all Started

Why do we fix your Flask? this is why

An average high schooler with some average spray paint decided to give his old water bottle a makeover, drawing the attention of all of his peers. 4 years later, being entered in art fairs, holding demonstrations, and upgrading some equipment, he worked toward making sure nobody had to throw away their bottle ever again.

The environment needed saving, and so did the people! Our mission is to beautify the old and give to the needy. Everyone deserves to have a reliable source of water, and there's no reason it can't be sexy!
